Climate Change and Nature

Climate change is one of the biggest threats facing the natural world today. Rising temperatures, changing weather patterns, and other effects of climate change are already having a profound impact on ecosystems and species around the world.

Scientists predict that these impacts will only get worse in the years to come, unless we take urgent action to reduce our greenhouse gas emissions and transition to a more sustainable and climate-friendly economy.

If we don't act now, we risk losing some of the most beautiful and valuable parts of our natural world, from coral reefs and rainforests to polar ice caps and endangered species.
